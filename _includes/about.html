About Pixel Outlaw

Pixel Outlaw is a company founded by Richard Harrah and Kyle Kattrein for the sole purpose of becoming a haven of dank memes and creating fun gameplay experiences.

It coalesced around Faceland, a Minecraft server still run by the founders. It then transformed into Teal Cube Games, where a single Android game was created, earning Richard and Kyle $5 and a sense of pride. Of course, creating the game and putting it on the Android store cost $25, but that's not the point of this paragraph.

Pixel Outlaw's current projects include the continuation of Faceland, the development of a new website for Faceland, and multiple half-hearted attempts at making the games that we so desperately want to make but don't have the time or energy to complete. We also browse dank memes frequently.
